About Me

Greyson Wei (魏龙) is now a talent acquisition professional at Covalent Careers, specializing in recruiting for AI, quantitative finance, and venture capital across Mainland China, Hong Kong (SAR), and Singapore.

Greyson holds dual B.S. degrees in Economics from M.V. Lomonosov Moscow State University and Shenzhen MSU-BIT University (2022–2026), where he was advised by Prof. Oksana V. Kuchmaeva (Demography, Faculty of Economics). Previously, he was an HR Assistant at Lighthouse Capital, where he built an AI-based resume parsing tool and maintained talent pools for LLM research and frontier healthcare.

He speaks Mandarin, English, and Russian.
